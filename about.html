History of Astrology in the Poddar family

Bhartiya Jyotishalaya is the foundation of my late father Uma Prasad Ji Poddar. An avid visionary in his own right. Shri Uma Prasad Ji Poddar started practicing astrology in 1938 in Mumbai. He extensively studied astrology in Banaras Hindu Vishwa Vidyalaya (BHU). After successfully practicing astrology for about 6 years in Mumbai, he shifted to Jabalpur and therefore started an extraordinary jounrey of knowledge and success in the field of astrology. His astounding legacy is carried forward by his son Sharad Uma Prasad Poddar.
